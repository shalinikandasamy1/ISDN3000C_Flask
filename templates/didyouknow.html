Did you know?
Under HKUST’s towering red “bird” sundial stands a 7‑meter stone carving showing 39 ancient Chinese inventions – from iron plows and seismographs to early rockets.
Purpose
Created in 1991 by artist Alan Chung Hung Smith, it celebrates China’s long tradition of innovation at a brand‑new technology university.
Symbolism
The “Circle of Time” design links ancient genius to modern discovery, reminding HKUST students that they are part of that continuum every day.
